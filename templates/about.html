About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is a Machine Learning-based Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine designed to recommend the most suitable crop based on soil nutrients and climatic conditions.

The system analyzes important agricultural parameters including Nitrogen (N), Phosphorous (P), Potassium (K), Temperature, Humidity, pH, and Rainfall to generate intelligent crop recommendations.

By combining Artificial Intelligence with modern agricultural practices, OptiCrop helps farmers improve productivity, reduce crop failure, optimize resource utilization, and support sustainable farming.
