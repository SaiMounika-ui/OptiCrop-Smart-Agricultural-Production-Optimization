# Specify the Business Problem

## Overview

Agriculture is a critical sector that plays a vital role in ensuring food security and supporting the economy. However, farmers often face challenges in selecting the most suitable crop due to variations in soil composition, climatic conditions, rainfall patterns, and seasonal changes. Incorrect crop selection can result in poor agricultural productivity, inefficient utilization of resources, increased cultivation costs, and financial losses.

## Problem Statement

The primary business problem addressed by the **OptiCrop: Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine** is the lack of an intelligent decision-support system that assists farmers in selecting the most appropriate crop based on soil nutrients and environmental conditions.

Traditional farming practices rely heavily on manual experience and historical knowledge, which may not accurately reflect current environmental conditions. This often leads to reduced crop yields and inefficient agricultural planning.

## Business Need

The proposed system analyzes the following agricultural parameters:

- Nitrogen (N)
- Phosphorus (P)
- Potassium (K)
- Temperature
- Humidity
- Soil pH
- Rainfall
- Seasonal Conditions

Based on these parameters, the system recommends the most suitable crop for cultivation.

## Stakeholders

- Farmers
- Agricultural Researchers
- Agribusiness Organizations
- Government Agricultural Departments
- Policymakers

## Business Objectives

- Develop an AI-based crop recommendation system.
- Improve crop productivity.
- Optimize resource utilization.
- Reduce crop failure risks.
- Promote sustainable agriculture.

## Expected Outcomes

- Accurate crop recommendations.
- Increased agricultural productivity.
- Better decision-making.
- Efficient use of soil nutrients and water.
- Sustainable farming practices.
